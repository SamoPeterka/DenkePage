About Denile

Beside musical career, I'm Phd student of Philosophy at University of Ljubljana, researching Neoplatonism, modern hermeneutics and German idealism. Philosophy made strong impact on my analytic skills that are a necessity for complex orchestral score. Every tune needs the right character which derives from deeper understanding of conceptual background. Music is not just an art, but also form of logic and cosmic order.

During professional career, I worked as an audiodesigner, film score composer, mixing engineer, producer, arranger and songwriter. Even though I'm specialized for metal and orchestra, working in field of pop, jazz, rock or electronic music is no problem for me. Every producer must be familiar with wide variety of genres and ethnic traditions that fall under the flag of »world spirit«.

During professional career, I worked as an audiodesigner, film score composer, mixing engineer, producer, arranger and songwriter. Even though I'm specialized for metal and orchestra, working in field of pop, jazz, rock or electronic music is no problem for me. Every producer must be familiar with wide variety of genres and ethnic traditions that fall under the flag of »world spirit«. Since 2016 I've been part of Negligence Studio, leading metal&rock recording studio in Ljubljana, however you can hire me as a freelancer. I am always ready to travel and work with you at your location.


Since I have no formal education in classical composition, I learned during collaborating with renowned composers such as Anže Rozman (hollywood film score composer at Bleeding fingers music) and Dame Tomoski (Macedonian film score and orchestra composer).

My journey as a musician began in 2009, when I founded Condemnatio Cristi. After years of touring with my bands, I decided to remain active only as a studio musician and producer, since more and more people were asking for my services. I had honour to collaborate with outstanding musicians such as Mike Smith (ex. Suffocation) Robert Kovačič (ex. Belphegor), Simon Škrlec (Melechesh), Tony Choy (ex. Pestilence, ex. Cynic), Adrie Kloosterward (Sinister), James Mcintyre (Voracious Scourge, ex.Suture) and many more…
