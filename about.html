About me

Beside musical career, I'm Phd student of Philosophy at University of Ljubljana, researching Neoplatonism, modern hermeneutics and German Idealism. Philosophy made a strong impact on my analytical skills, that are a necessity for every complex production. Each tune needs the right character which derives from deeper understanding of conceptual background. Music is not just an art, but also form of logic and cosmic order.

During professional career I worked as an audiodesigner, film score composer, mixing&recording engineer, arranger and songwriter. Since 2016 I'm part of Negligence Studio, leading metal recording&mixing facility in Ljubljana, however you can also hire me as a freelancer. I am always ready to travel and work with you at your location.

Since I have no formal education in classical composition, I mastered my skills during collaborating with renowned composers such as Anže Rozman (hollywood film score composer at Bleeding fingers music), and Dame Tomoski (film score and orchestra composer).

My musical journey began in 2009, when I founded Condemnatio Cristi. After years of touring with my bands, I decided to remain active only as a studio musician and producer, since more and more people were asking for my services. I had honour to collaborate with outstanding musicians such as Mike Smith (ex. Suffocation), Tony Choy (ex. Pestilence, Cynic), Adrie Kloosterward (Sinister), Jason Mcintyre (Voracious Scourge, Suture), Robert Kovačič (ex. Belphegor), Simon Škrlec (Melechesh) and many more...
